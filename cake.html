Cake

A cake is a sweet or savory dish made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients, that is usually baked or cooked in a round shape. In its oldest forms, cakes were served in pies, cakes, and pancakes. In the modern world, cakes are often baked and decorated with frosting, fruit, and other toppings. Cakes can be made with a wide range of ingredients, including flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and other ingredients. The most popular cake recipes include chocolate cake, cheesecake, and pound cake. Some cakes are also prepared with other ingredients, such as nuts, fruit, or vegetables. Cakes are often served with ice cream, whipped cream, or other desserts.
